--- type: post layout: main title: "To Do: Integrate Jott and Remember the Milk" --- I've been using Remember the Milk for a little while now and have been pretty pleased with the results. Today I stumbled across a service called Jott, which transcribes voice into text and can emails you the result. Two obvious uses: voicemails and transforming your phone into a personal voice recorder/to-do list generator. Since Jott will send an email to any address you specify as a contact, (e.g. your Remember the Milk inbox address), it's pretty easy to integrate the two yourself by hand; however, poking around their blog revealed that the smart people over there have already thought of that and have automated the process. Kudos.
